Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Tomato slices
  • Butter
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1. Cook bacon until crispy.
  • 2. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  • 3. Place one slice of bread, butter-side down, in a skillet over medium heat.
  • 4. Add cheddar cheese on top of the bread slice in the skillet.
  • 5. Add tomato slices and cooked bacon on top of the cheese.
  • 6. Place the second bread slice on top, butter-side up.
  • 7. Cook until the bottom slice is golden brown, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.
  • 8. Remove from skillet and serve hot.
