Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bacon (6 slices)
  • Cheddar cheese (4 slices)
  • Butter (2 tablespoons)
  • Tomato (1, sliced)
  • Bread (4 slices)

Instructions:

1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.

2. Cook bacon until crispy, then remove from skillet and drain on paper towels.

3. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.

4. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of a slice of bread, then add bacon slices, tomato slices, and another slice of cheese. Top with another slice of bread, buttered side facing out.

5. Repeat with remaining ingredients to make another sandwich.

6. Place sandwiches in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides and the cheese is melted.

7. Serve hot and enjoy!
